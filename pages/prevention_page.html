To prevent IPV, improving the level of security of devices is essential.

Set up a password for your device that only you know.

1. Avoid: birth dates (0120, if your birthday is January 20th), simple series of number(1234, 4321) repetitions. (0000, 1111)

2. Log out of accounts and apps so that if others do gain access to the device, they can't get the content in the apps.

3. Be aware of who has access to your device and watch out for any unfamiliar application in your phone that you didn't install.
